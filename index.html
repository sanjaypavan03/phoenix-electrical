Process

Advanced Manufacturing. Proven Results.

An overview of the insulator production process at Phoenix. Through our specialized products and procedures, we position ourselves much ahead of manufacturing facilities available in India.

01
FRP Manufacturing

Using Pultrusion & Pullwinding machines to manufacture high-quality FRP rods. Raw materials sourced from global markets for optimal quality.

02
Preparation & Bonding

FRP rods are cleaned and bonding agent is applied. After 25–35 minutes, they proceed to the metal fittings crimping process.

03
Rubber Processing

Crimped FRP rods are placed inside the die and silicone compound is charged over it. After the desired curing time, components are ejected from the die.

04
Testing & Dispatch

Insulators are tested for visual inspection, crimping strength, and high voltage testing. Packed and kept in storage area for dispatch.

Process Chart Overview

The basic elements involved are FRP rod, METAL fittings, SILICONE rubber. The basic machines involved are PULTRUSION & PULLWINDING machine for FRP manufacturing, CRIMPING with AE detector machine for METAL fittings crimping, RUBBER processing machine & MOLD for molding.

Pultrusion & Pullwinding

High-tech process for manufacturing strong, consistent FRP rods with precise dimensional tolerances suitable for insulator applications.

Crimping

Metal fittings crimping with Acoustic Emission (AE) detector machine ensures superior mechanical performance and reliability.

Rubber Process

Silicone compound molding ensures excellent weathering resistance, hydrophobicity, and long-term electrical performance.

Testing

Comprehensive quality verification including visual inspection, crimp strength testing, and high-voltage electrical testing.
